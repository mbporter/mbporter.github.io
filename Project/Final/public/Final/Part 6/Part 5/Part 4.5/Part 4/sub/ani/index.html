Zoolandia

Welcome to Mac’s new Zoo named Zoolandia, the happiest place for animal lovers and conservation enthusiasts!

At Zoolandia, we’re not just a zoo; we’re a vibrant community dedicated to supporting animals and championing conservation. Our goal is to educate and inspire visitors of all ages with our interactive exhibits and engaging educational programs. We believe that learning about animals is the first step towards loving and protecting them.

But wait, there’s more! Can’t make it to Zoolandia today? No problem! We’ve got you covered with our live animal feeds. Tune in from the comfort of your home and watch our furry friends in real-time as they play, nap, and show off their unique personalities. It’s all the fun without the need for sunscreen!

So come on down, bring a smile, and join us in our mission to make the world a better place for all our animal friends. Zoolandia is where the wild things are… and they’re waiting to meet you!
